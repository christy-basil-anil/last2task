A universe of super heroes

Marvel Comics, founded in 1939 as Timely Publications, has become one of the most influential and iconic entertainment companies in the world. Known for its vast universe of superheroes and supervillains, Marvel introduced some of the most beloved characters in popular culture, including Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, the X-Men, and the Avengers. With its interconnected storytelling across comics, television, and film, Marvel has built a shared universe that captivates audiences globally. The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), launched in 2008 with Iron Man, revolutionized modern cinema, creating a blueprint for successful interconnected franchises. Marvel's impact extends beyond entertainment, with its themes of heroism, diversity, and moral complexity resonating with fans of all ages.
